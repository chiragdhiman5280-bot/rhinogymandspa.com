Diet Plans

Each plan keeps meals simple, repeatable and easy to follow for gym members.

Fat Loss

Calorie-Control Plan

For members aiming to reduce fat while keeping strength stable.

  • High-protein breakfast
  • Lean protein with vegetables at lunch and dinner
  • Controlled rice, roti or potato portions
  • Limit fried snacks, sugary drinks and late-night overeating
Muscle Gain

Lean Bulk Plan

For members who want to add muscle without unnecessary fat gain.

  • Protein in every meal
  • Extra carbs around workout time
  • Milk, curd, eggs, paneer, chicken or dal as protein options
  • Track body weight weekly and increase food slowly
Maintenance

Balanced Lifestyle Plan

For members who want fitness, energy and a sustainable routine.

  • 3 main meals and 1 protein snack
  • Balanced portions of protein, carbs and fats
  • Hydration target of 2.5 to 3.5 liters daily
  • Flexible meals while keeping weekly consistency
Vegetarian

Vegetarian Protein Plan

For vegetarian members who need enough protein for training.

  • Paneer, tofu, soya, dal, chana and curd
  • Protein spread across 4 meals
  • Fruit before training when energy is low
  • Use whey only if it fits your preference and budget
Busy Members

Simple Meal Prep Plan

For members with work, college or limited cooking time.

  • Repeatable breakfast and lunch options
  • Carry fruit, nuts, curd or protein snack
  • Pre-cook protein for 2 to 3 days
  • Keep dinner lighter on non-training days
Recovery

Recovery Support Plan

For members training hard or rebuilding energy and sleep quality.

  • Enough carbs after intense sessions
  • Protein within daily target
  • Electrolytes and water during hot days
  • Consistent sleep schedule with lighter late meals

Simple Plate Guide

Protein Eggs, paneer, chicken, fish, dal, chana, tofu or soya
Carbs Rice, roti, oats, potato, fruit or poha
Fats Nuts, seeds, ghee, olive oil or peanut butter
Micros Vegetables, salad, fruit and enough water
