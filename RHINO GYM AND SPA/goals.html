Body Goals By Member Type

Choose a realistic target and review it every 4 weeks with your coach.

Starter

Build The Habit

Best for new members who need consistency before chasing advanced targets.

  • Train 3 days each week
  • Learn basic machines and movement patterns
  • Eat protein in 3 meals daily
  • Track attendance first, then weight changes
Fat Loss

Lose Weight And Tone Up

Best for members who want a leaner look and better stamina.

  • Strength training plus regular cardio
  • Moderate calorie deficit
  • Waist, weight and progress photos every 2 weeks
  • Target: 0.5 to 1 kg change per week
Lean Muscle

Gain Shape Without Bulk

Best for members who want muscle tone, posture and visible definition.

  • 4 to 5 strength sessions weekly
  • Small calorie surplus or maintenance intake
  • High protein and consistent sleep
  • Track lifts, measurements and mirror progress
Muscle Size

Bulk With Control

Best for members who want size and are ready to train hard consistently.

  • 5 to 6 training days weekly
  • Progressive overload on main exercises
  • Calorie surplus with clean food choices
  • Target: steady weight gain, not fast fat gain
Strength

Get Stronger

Best for members who care most about performance and lifting numbers.

  • Focus on compound lifts
  • Planned rest and recovery days
  • Enough carbs before heavy sessions
  • Track personal bests and form quality
Wellness

Feel Fit And Healthy

Best for members who want energy, mobility, confidence and stress control.

  • Mix strength, cardio and flexibility
  • Balanced diet with hydration
  • Recovery, spa and sleep focus
  • Track energy, mood and routine consistency
