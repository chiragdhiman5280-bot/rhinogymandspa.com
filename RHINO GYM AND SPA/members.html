Workout Splits And Diet Plans By Goal

Choose the plan that matches your current goal and review progress with your Rhino coach.

Beginners

Beginner Foundation

Workout Split

  • Monday: Full body machines
  • Tuesday: Rest or light walk
  • Wednesday: Full body strength
  • Thursday: Mobility and cardio
  • Friday: Full body technique
  • Weekend: Recovery

Diet Plan

  • Protein in every main meal
  • Simple home meals with roti, rice, dal, eggs, paneer or chicken
  • Fruit before training when energy is low
  • 2.5 to 3 liters water daily
Fat Loss

Fat Loss And Toning

Workout Split

  • Monday: Upper body plus cardio
  • Tuesday: Lower body plus core
  • Wednesday: Cardio intervals
  • Thursday: Push and pull training
  • Friday: Legs plus steady cardio
  • Saturday: Mobility and abs

Diet Plan

  • Moderate calorie deficit
  • High protein breakfast
  • Lean protein with vegetables at lunch and dinner
  • Limit fried food, sugar drinks and late-night snacks
Muscle Gain

Bodybuilding Split

Workout Split

  • Monday: Chest and triceps
  • Tuesday: Back and biceps
  • Wednesday: Legs
  • Thursday: Shoulders and abs
  • Friday: Arms and weak points
  • Saturday: Pump workout or recovery cardio

Diet Plan

  • Small calorie surplus
  • Protein target across 4 meals
  • Carbs around workout time
  • Track body weight and increase food slowly
Strength

Strength Focus

Workout Split

  • Monday: Heavy push
  • Tuesday: Heavy legs
  • Wednesday: Rest and mobility
  • Thursday: Heavy pull
  • Friday: Full body strength
  • Weekend: Recovery and stretching

Diet Plan

  • Balanced meals with enough carbs
  • Protein with every meal
  • Hydration and salt before heavy sessions
  • Prioritize sleep and recovery foods
Overall Fitness

Overall Fitness Plan

Workout Split

  • Monday: Full body strength
  • Tuesday: Cardio and core
  • Wednesday: Upper body
  • Thursday: Mobility and recovery
  • Friday: Lower body
  • Saturday: Conditioning circuit

Diet Plan

  • Balanced protein, carbs and healthy fats
  • Vegetables or salad with two meals
  • Consistent meal timing
  • Flexible weekend meal while staying on track
