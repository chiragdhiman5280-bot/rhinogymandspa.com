Workout Plans

Select the track that matches the member profile closest to you.

New Members

Beginner Foundation

For members starting fresh or returning after a long break.

  • 3 days strength training
  • 2 days light cardio
  • Full-body machines and basic form work
  • Goal: confidence, consistency and safe progress
Fat Loss

Fat Loss Engine

For members focused on dropping body fat and improving stamina.

  • 4 days resistance training
  • 3 cardio finishers each week
  • Core work after alternate sessions
  • Goal: calorie burn, muscle tone and discipline
Muscle Gain

Muscle Builder

For members who want size, shape and stronger lifts.

  • 5 day push, pull, legs split
  • Progressive overload tracking
  • Compound lifts plus isolation work
  • Goal: lean mass, strength and symmetry
Strength

Strength Focus

For intermediate members who want measurable power gains.

  • 4 heavy lifting days
  • Squat, press, row and hinge focus
  • Longer rest periods and lower reps
  • Goal: stronger basics and better performance
Busy Schedule

3-Day Transformation

For members who can train only three focused days per week.

  • 3 full-body sessions
  • Short cardio blocks after training
  • Supersets to save time
  • Goal: maximum return from limited sessions
Recovery

Mobility And Recovery

For members rebuilding movement quality or balancing intense training.

  • 2 mobility sessions
  • Low-impact cardio
  • Stretching and controlled tempo work
  • Goal: better joints, posture and recovery

Exercise Splits By Body Goal

Simple workout splits members can follow according to their current goal.

Beginners

3 Day Full Body

Best for learning form and building consistency.

  • Day 1: Leg press, chest press, lat pulldown, shoulder press, plank
  • Day 2: Squat basics, seated row, dumbbell press, curls, treadmill walk
  • Day 3: Full body machines, core work and stretching
  • Sets: 2 to 3 sets of 12 to 15 reps
Fat Loss

4 Day Fat Loss Split

Strength training with cardio finishers for calorie burn.

  • Day 1: Upper body plus 15 minutes cardio
  • Day 2: Lower body plus abs
  • Day 3: Full body circuit and cycling
  • Day 4: Push, pull, legs mix plus incline walk
  • Keep rest short and movement clean
Bodybuilding

5 Day Muscle Split

Best for members focused on muscle gain and shape.

  • Monday: Chest and triceps
  • Tuesday: Back and biceps
  • Wednesday: Legs and calves
  • Thursday: Shoulders and abs
  • Friday: Arms and weak points
  • Sets: 3 to 4 sets of 8 to 12 reps
Strength

4 Day Strength Split

Simple heavy days with enough recovery.

  • Day 1: Chest press, shoulder press, triceps
  • Day 2: Squat or leg press, hamstrings, calves
  • Day 3: Lat pulldown, rows, biceps
  • Day 4: Full body heavy basics
  • Sets: 4 sets of 5 to 8 reps
Overall Fitness

Balanced Weekly Split

For members who want strength, stamina and mobility together.

  • Monday: Full body strength
  • Tuesday: Cardio and core
  • Wednesday: Upper body
  • Thursday: Mobility and recovery
  • Friday: Lower body
  • Saturday: Conditioning circuit
Weight Gain

Lean Bulk Split

Train hard, recover well and increase food slowly.

  • Day 1: Push exercises
  • Day 2: Pull exercises
  • Day 3: Legs
  • Day 4: Rest
  • Day 5: Upper body pump
  • Day 6: Legs and core

Weekly Training Guide

Beginner 3 to 4 days per week
Fat Loss 4 to 5 days per week
Muscle Gain 5 to 6 days per week
Strength 4 days per week
